DairyVue360 is a one for all solution for the wellbeing of your herd. It offers many unique solutions in the domain of Herd Fertility, Milking and Health. It consists of a Mobile App and Web Dashboard that helps farmers connect with other physical Dairymaster products that own in their farm to access data in real-time.

This Case Study only deals with the Milking Section on the DairyVue360 Mobile App.A similar Design Process was followed to design other pages of the Mobile App and Web Dashboard.



The Challenges


Challenge 1

While DairyVue360 is being used across all levels of Farming, yet companies like Dairymaster are innovation leaders in the field to discover, invent and develop new product and features.The research required to conduct UX studies is dependant on field experts rather than end users themselves. The field experts who work with us have farming experience themselves and hence provide a good perspective on the product problems as well as innovation ideas.


Challenge 2

DairyVue360 is an expert level software with wide, specific and niche features hence it becomes more challenging to define the design vision in such a way that makes it as easy as possible for every farmer to use it with the least amount of training.


Challenge 3

We offer our App and Web Dashboard in several countries across the globe. This introduces challenges like cultural/metric differences in farming practices. We also havefarms varying in size from 10 animals to a 10,000 animals in a few farms.
